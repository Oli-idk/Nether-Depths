Features

AngelChests

When you die, your items are safely added into a chest at your death location. You can also do /actp to tp there!

ChestShop

Sell anything for any price with Chest Shops at your own custom warp with /setwarp or at /warp shops!

Rideable Mobs

You can ride any mob with a special token you can get from vote crates! Including flying squids!

Claims

Use a Golden Shovel to claim your base and prevent grief. You can also play around with various claim flags.

Discord

Please join the Discord for important updates! All announcements are here
netherdepths.com/discord

Vote Crates

Vote with /vote and get crate keys for crates that give you many different rewards like armor, money, and more!

GSit

Sit, lay or crawl anywhere with /sit, /crawl, and /lay. You can also right click stairs with a free hand to sit!

HorseTpWithMe

You can teleport any rideable mob with you while riding it, useful if you want to transport villagers in boats.

Marriage

Love someone so much that you want to marry them in the server? Marry them with /marry [player]!

Silk Spawners

Mine monster spawners and create monster grinders or farms easily with a Silk Touch pickaxe!

BottledExp

Want to transfer your XP or give it to others? Do /bottle get [amount] to transfer your XP to bottles!

DrSleep

Easily skip the night with the need of only 1 player to sleep! Want to keep the night? do /nosleep!

Claim Flags

Claim flags can be used to alter the environment of your claims, you can get different claim flags while ranking up.

Chat Races

Earn money for racing others in chat, answering various questions, unscrambling words, and more!

mcMMO

Get experience in different aspects of Minecraft and create your own party with a group of your friends!

PvPToggle

Tired of PvPing and don't want others to kill you? do /pvp off to disable PvP!

Creative Plots

Tired of Survival or just want to build something to get your mind off of things? Do /warp creative!

Minigames

Play various minigames with your friends: Spleef, Last Man Standing, Elytra Run, Parkour, and more soon!

Jobs

Do various jobs to earn money with /jobs and get money from doing different actions in the server!

Donation Perks

You can donate to get some perks like /stack, /condense, custom armor stands, and more! More information here
